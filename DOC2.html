Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of equipment do I need for online consultation?

A: You will need an internet-connected device with a camera and microphone. This could be a smartphone, tablet, or computer. Make sure your device is charged and you have a stable internet connection for the best experience.

Q: Are online veterinary consultations safe and private?

A: Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security. All consultations are conducted over a secure, encrypted connection to ensure the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

Q: Can a veterinarian diagnose and prescribe medications online?

A: Veterinarians can provide an initial assessment and advice online. In some cases, they may be able to prescribe medications. However, some cases may require a physical examination or diagnostic tests to make an accurate diagnosis and prescription.
